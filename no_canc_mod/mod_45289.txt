She had developed leukocytosis, abdominal pain, hypotension and lactic acidosis.
She also was also in atrial fibrillation with heart rates to the 170s.
She was converted into normal sinus rhythm with amiodarone; however her hypotension and acidosis did not improve.
She had been on antibiotics and there was concern for c difficile colitis.
Her family declined autopsy but the cause of the decline leading to her death was felt to have been most likely bowel infarction in the setting of atrial fibrillation.
