The patient was treated with Octreotide for five days intravenously and patient's hematocrit stabilized after approximately five units of packed red blood cells.
Patient was treated with Flagyl and Lactulose with return of his mental status back to baseline which is alert and oriented times three.
This is consistent with spontaneous bacterial peritonitis, thus patient was started on Ceftriaxone 2 grams IV q. day.
The patient's acute renal failure resolved with hydration with his creatinine trending back down towards baseline.
However, patient had markedly increased need for oxygen during the course of his hospitalization requiring up to 15 liters face mask to maintain saturations over 90%.
Patient was discharged home with adequate Ativan, Morphine and a Scopolamine patch for palliative care.
