The patient tolerated the procedure well, but remained intubated and sedated overnight in the Trauma SICU in order to maintain hemodynamic stability.
Patient's nasogastric tube was also removed at this time.
On postoperative day two, the patient was tachycardic and tachypneic, therefore chest x-ray was obtained which was basically unchanged from the preoperative chest x-ray indicating no need on postoperative diuresis.
Patient's IV fluids were decreased.
He received a Physical Therapy evaluation on postoperative day #4 as he had three flights of stairs at his home.
His staples were removed prior to discharge.
