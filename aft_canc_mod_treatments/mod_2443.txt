On [**5-14**] the patient started to spike temperatures and had increased somnolence as well as increased diarrhea.
She was placed on Vancomycin, Levofloxacin and Flagyl to cover GI, lung, and line as possible sources.
Neo-Synephrine was weaned off within 24 hours with aggressive volume resuscitation.
The patient completed a 14 day course of Vancomycin on [**5-26**] for presumed MRSA pneumonia.
The Levofloxacin and Flagyl were discontinued on [**5-21**] as the patient's diarrhea was much improved.
Vancomycin was restarted at this time for a recurrent MRSA pneumonia.
Lopressor was initiated and titrated up during the MICU course with improvement in these findings.
She was on TPN throughout the admission.
