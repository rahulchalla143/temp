The patient underwent a rigid cystoscopy in order to place a Foley catheter.
An EKG was done and was normal.
His electrolytes were managed and this spontaneously resolved.
As his calcium started to drop further, he was started on calcium twice a day, as well as Rocaltrol 0.5 mcg daily.
