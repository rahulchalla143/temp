SUMMARY OF HOSPITAL COURSE BY SYSTEMS:  RESPIRATORY:  The infant was admitted to the newborn intensive care unit for grunting, flaring, retracting.
She was placed on CPAP and further progressed to needing intubation.
She was intubated and received one dose of Surfactant.
Enteral feedings were started on day of life one.
The infant has not required any blood transfusions.
The infant received ampicillin and gentamicin for a total of 48 hours at which time antibiotics were discontinued with a negative blood culture.
CARE RECOMMENDATIONS:  Feeds at discharge: Continue ad lib enteral feeding of breast milk or Similac 20 calorie.
IMMUNIZATIONS RECEIVED:  Hepatitis B immunization was administered prior to discharge.
